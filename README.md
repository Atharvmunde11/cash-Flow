# CashFlow

**Free accounting software for small Indian businesses — runs on your machine, not in the cloud.**

CashFlow helps you manage invoices, purchases, customers, stock, and payments in one place. Your data stays on your PC in a local database. No signup, no subscription, no internet required after install.

---

## Download CashFlow

**Don't clone or build this repository to use CashFlow.**

Go to our download page to get the Windows installer (`.exe`) for your computer:

**[Download CashFlow →](https://opensource.lucareo.com/cashflow)**

From there you can download:

- **Windows x64** — for Intel and AMD PCs
- **Windows ARM64** — for Surface and other ARM Windows laptops

One-click install. No account needed. Completely free.

---

## Why CashFlow?

- **100% local** — your books never leave your machine
- **Works offline** — full app without internet after install
- **Free forever** — no trial, no monthly fee
- **Built for small businesses** — simple accounting if you don't deal in GST
- **Tally & BUSY import** — bring over customers, stock, invoices, and vouchers

---

## What you can do

- Create invoices and purchase bills (with PDF export)
- Track customers, suppliers, payments, and money owed
- Manage inventory and low-stock alerts
- View revenue, collections, and category breakdowns on a dashboard
- Import data from **Tally** or **BUSY**

---

## Import from Tally or BUSY

1. Open **Settings → Import from Tally or BUSY** in the app
2. Export from your current software:
   - **BUSY:** `MSAll` (masters) + `Vh` (vouchers)
   - **Tally:** XML export (masters and vouchers)
3. Upload the files and choose **Merge** or **Replace**
